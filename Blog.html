5 Tips for Taking Care of Indoor Plants

  • Place the plants in a location that receives adequate light.
  • Do not water the plants excessively; only water when the soil is dry
  • Wipe the leaves regularly to remove dust.
  • Use a natural fertilizer monthly.
  • Monitor the leaves for any signs of disease or yellowing.

How to Choose the Right Plant for Your Space

  • Type of lighting (natural, low)
  • Available space (corners, offices, balconies)
  • Time you can devote to care
  • The aesthetic look you desire
